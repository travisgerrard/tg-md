Three important things in diastolic HF

NMDA

  • N-methyl-D-aspartate is a neurotrasmitter that acts at the receptor.
  • Anti-NMDA receptor encephalitis is on diffential of patient with encephalitis

Hidradenitis Suppurativa

  • Inflammatory disease around abnormal and dilated hair follicles with secondary involvement of apocrine glands and surrounding structures, often with superinfection.
  • Tx
      Moist warm heat compresses 4-6 x /day.
      Doxycycline 100mg PO twice-daily
      Surgery with wide excision is the only curative treatment.

Imipenem/Cilastatin

  • Side effects: Seizures (elderly), LFT elevation RARE: bone marrow suppresion

CSF findings: bacteral vs viral

  • Bacterial: WBC > 2000, protein > 200, glucose < 20
  • Viral: WBC < 300, protein < 150, glucose ~ 40 (normal)
